Portfolio

Mechanical Meanderings

Mechanical Meanderings is an interactive installation that explores embodiement of metaphors and self-perception. Participants speak words which are analyzed by AI systems that formats their query into text-to-image prompts. These prompts are used in real-time with the camera capturing movements, blending their silhouettes with their metaphors. The installation is a solo project of mine, currently in development. It was exhbited at MUTEK Montréal 2024 and at MTL Connect 2024.

Meteomythosophy

Meteomythosophy is a transdisciplinary framework for immersive performances, connecting neuro-computational arts, religious studies, and laboratory theater. It uses meteorological metaphors to express the fluid and unpredictable aspects of consciousness and sense-making. The project was showcased at Embodied Interventions Festival 2024. It is a collaboration between Paolo Gruni, Thomas Seibel and I.
